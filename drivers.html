Our Drivers



Pierre Gasly

French sensation Pierre Gasly, born on February 7, 1996, in Rouen, has made a name for himself in the Formula One world with his tenacity and skill. His journey from karting at the age of six to his triumphant win at the 2020 Italian Grand Prix is a testament to his dedication. Currently racing for Alpine, Gasly's career highlights include a GP2 Series Championship in 2016 and several podium finishes in F1, showcasing his consistent performance and competitive spirit.

Joining together

During the 2023 Formula One season, Pierre Gasly joined Esteban Ocon at Alpine, creating an all-French driver lineup that sparked much enthusiasm within the team and among fans. Gasly, transitioning from AlphaTauri, was eager to integrate into a team with deep French roots and was welcomed with open arms, heralded for his proven talent and alignment with Alpine's ambitious progress. Alpine's management expressed confidence in the synergistic potential of Gasly and Ocon, aiming to utilize their combined experience and motivation to propel the team towards its lofty objectives, emphasizing the symbolic significance of a French lineup driving the team forward.
